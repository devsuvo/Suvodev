Common questions

Before you ask

How do payments work?

Typically 50% upfront to book the project and 50% on delivery. For larger projects we can split into milestones. I accept bank transfer, Wise, Payoneer and Fiverr.

What if I need changes after delivery?

Every package includes unlimited revisions during the project and a free support window after launch. After that, I offer affordable monthly maintenance.

How long does a project take?

Starter sites ship in about a week. Business builds usually take 2–3 weeks. Custom applications depend on scope — you'll get a clear timeline with your quote.

Do you work with clients outside Bangladesh?

Yes — most of my clients are in the US and Europe. I work remotely, communicate daily, and adjust to your timezone for calls.
