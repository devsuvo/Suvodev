How Much Does a Website Cost in Bangladesh? (2026 Guide)

pricing · Jan 12, 2026 · by Suvo Dev · Sylhet, Bangladesh

One of the most common questions business owners in Bangladesh ask is: how much should I budget for a website? The honest answer is — it depends on what the website needs to do. Here is a realistic breakdown from a developer who builds them every week.

Typical price ranges in 2026

  • Simple landing page (WordPress): $100–$300 — perfect for a small business that needs an online presence fast.
  • Business website (5–10 pages): $300–$800 — custom design, contact forms, SEO setup, and mobile optimization.
  • E-commerce store (Shopify/WooCommerce): $500–$2,000 — products, payments, shipping, and inventory.
  • Custom web application (React/Next.js): $1,500+ — dashboards, user accounts, and custom business logic.

What affects the price?

Three things drive cost more than anything else: custom design (versus a template), the number of features, and content. A site where you provide the text and photos will always cost less than one where the developer creates everything.

Where people waste money

The biggest mistake is paying for features you do not need yet. Start with a clean, fast website that clearly explains what you do, then add features as your business grows. A $300 website that loads in two seconds will outperform a $3,000 website that loads in eight.
